# Competitor Platform Intelligence

## FastMoss (fastmoss.com)
- **What they have:** TikTok Shop analytics, product sales data, creator commerce performance, ad analytics, 250M+ creators, 1200 days historical data
- **What we need:** Creator commerce performance (GMV, sales), trending product data, ad performance metrics
- **Access:** MantaYaY paid subscription
- **Useful for KolBuff:** 
  - Commerce-active creator identification (who's selling on TikTok Shop)
  - Revenue estimates for creators
  - Product category performance by creator
  - Ad creative performance data

## Kalodata (kalodata.com) 
- **What they have:** TikTok Shop product/creator/video analytics, 300M+ data points, 1000 days historical, creator sales estimates
- **Access:** MantaYaY paid subscription
- **Useful for KolBuff:**
  - Creator product promotion frequency and performance
  - Shop performance data
  - Category trend analysis
  - Creator commission/sales estimates

## Data Enrichment Strategy
1. Cross-reference their creator databases with ours
2. Extract commerce metrics we can't scrape directly (GMV, sales, commissions)
3. Add "commerce score" to creator profiles
4. Use trending product data for blog content (SEO)
5. Identify high-performing affiliates not in our DB yet
